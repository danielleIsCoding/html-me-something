Born to Game

Gaming Baby

Ive been gaming since I was about 3 years old, thats right I was gaming as a baby and everyone was shocked my little three year old hands knew what they were doing. I remember the very first video game I ever played was Mario. Yeah, I'm old I know, but I have a love for gaming. Being able to go into a different world is fun and relaxing. Since then, I've always been into playing video games and even watching other gamers play as well. You would think with all this experience I would be a pro gamer, but Im no where close. I game for the fun and not for the competition, so I'm just ok and thats good enough. I play a variety of game types and cycle through different ones depending on how I feel. Right now my game of choice is Call of Duty.

Current Obbsession...Call of Duty Cold War

I tried Call of Duty for the first time in 4 years ago and I hated it. I didn't see the fun it at all. I wasn't very good and I spent most of my time being beaten by better players. After that I didn't try again until Nov 2020. I gave it a chance because I bought a new gaming system and it was the only game released at the time that was optimized for the new system. To my surprise I enjoyed it and 4 months later its still number one.
